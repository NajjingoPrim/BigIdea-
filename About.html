About Our Initiative

Our Story

Urban Grind Initiative was founded in 2024 with a clear purpose: to combat poverty by addressing the pressing issue of unemployment in our community.

Our Mission

To empower the youth with practical skills to create a sustainable livelihood and break the poverty cycle.

Our Vision

A world with economically independent youths, empowered through skills and opportunity.

Our Objectives

  • Provide free training in soap making to unemployed youth
  • Offer comprehensive business development support
  • Implement environmentally friendly production methods

Our Services

  • Comprehensive soap making training program
  • Professional business mentorship
  • Market access and networking facilitation
No Poverty Initiative

Empowering youth through practical skills and sustainable solutions.
